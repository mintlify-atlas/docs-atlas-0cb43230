<Warning>

  - Production application traffic

## Risk Acknowledgment

By using this software, you acknowledge and accept the following:

### Terms of Service Risk

<Warning>
  This approach may violate the Terms of Service of AI model providers (Anthropic, Google, etc.). You are solely responsible for ensuring compliance with all applicable terms and policies.
</Warning>

The proxy works by:
- Authenticating with Google accounts to access Antigravity's Cloud Code service
- Translating between Anthropic and Google API formats
- Routing requests through unofficial channels

These methods may not be explicitly permitted by provider terms of service.

### Account Risk

<Warning>
  Providers may detect this usage pattern and take punitive action, including:
  - Account suspension or permanent ban
  - Loss of access to paid subscriptions (Pro/Ultra)
  - Revocation of API access
  - Financial penalties
</Warning>

**High-Risk Scenarios:**
- Using fresh Google accounts with no prior activity
- Immediately subscribing to Pro/Ultra and using the proxy
- Generating unusually high request volumes
- Rapid account switching or automation patterns

**Recommendations for Account Safety:**
- Use established Google accounts with normal activity history
- Start with low request volumes and gradually increase
- Avoid suspicious patterns (e.g., requests at exact intervals)
- Monitor accounts regularly for verification prompts
- Maintain normal Google account activity alongside proxy usage

### No Guarantees

<Warning>
  Providers may change APIs, authentication, or policies at any time, which can break this method without notice.
</Warning>

The proxy relies on:
- Undocumented API endpoints that may change or disappear
- OAuth flows that could be restricted
- Model availability that fluctuates based on quotas and policies

There is no guarantee of continued functionality.

### Assumption of Risk

<Warning>

</Warning>

**Use at your own risk. Proceed only if you understand and accept these risks.**
